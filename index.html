Tags Best Practices

Before using YouTube tags you should know some best practises:

1. Always use video related tags

Never use the tag which is not related to your video. For example if your video is about app development than you should use tags like app development, android development etc. Not like unboxing. This will not help you to rank your video.

If by chance your video ranked on YouTube for some keyword than users will not click on your video.

2. Use target keyword first

Always use the target keyword first. That simply means write the tag first for which your video is mostly related and you wanna rank for. It will definitely boost your video ranking on YouTube.

Then use some less related keywords afterwards.
